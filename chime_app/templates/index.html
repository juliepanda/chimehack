About

Think Twice For Change is a safe, anonymous, and non-confrontational way to let someone know that they are engaging in behaviors that are hostile towards women. Most men don’t set out to be threatening or reinforce rape culture, so a discrete heads up from someone they know has the power to make big difference. Unfortunately, both men and women routinely let these behaviors go, for fear of social or physical retaliation if they speak up. Pre-written messages sorted by behavior category empower observers to deploy information and raise awareness while placing the burden of change on the offender.
